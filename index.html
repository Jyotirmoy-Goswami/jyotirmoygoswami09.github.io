About Me

I am a plasma physics researcher with interests in low-temperature plasma, dusty plasma, ion acoustic waves, plasma instabilities, and experimental plasma diagnostics. My work involves filamentary discharge plasmas, negative ion plasmas, and plasma–dust interactions.

Research Interests

  • Low-temperature and filamentary discharge plasma
  • Dusty plasma and plasma fireball experiments
  • Ion acoustic waves and nonlinear structures
  • Negative ion plasma and wave dispersion
  • Plasma diagnostics (Langmuir probe, emissive probe, Surface Ionisation Detector, wave launching)
